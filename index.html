Websites for companies

Corporate websites are multi-page resources that provide information about the company and its services. The web designer is responsible for creating convenient navigation and a logical content structure, which contributes to better perception of information. The functionality of the site must meet the needs of the business.

Online Shops

Online stores allow you to sell products online. A web designer creates an intuitive interface, simplifying the search for products and the checkout process. The quality of product images and descriptions is also critical to attract customers and increase conversion.

Internet services

Online services (such as CRM or cloud storage) require user-friendly interfaces. A web designer focuses on ease of use and accessibility by creating interactive elements. Analytics helps identify problem areas in the interface to improve user experience.
